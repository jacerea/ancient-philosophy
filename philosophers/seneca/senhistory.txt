"I am Lucius Annaeus Seneca, I was born in Cordoba, Spain."
"I was taught the subjects of literature and rhetoric as a part of my education of high-born Romans."
"Due to this eduction, I become a Stoic philosopher of Ancient Rome as well as a statemen, dramatist, and a satirist, in Latin literature."
"I am most known for my works which include 12 essays and 124 letters dealing with moral issues."
"I love writing about tragedies and I believe that Stoicism is of the utmost importance in life."
